# XWind

The XWind application has been deployed at Airbus to support Flight Testing. This application aims at helping users to plan flight tests optimally by quickly identify airport runways matching specific business conditions (such as cross-wind) based on weather forecast data.

Watch the video

# Kano

The Kano application will be soon deployed at IRSN to support nuclear crisis management. This application aims at helping users to assess health risks optimally based on weather forecast data.
